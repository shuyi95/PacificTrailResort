Our History

The Pacific Trails Resorts has been established for over a decade, and we mainly persist to keep the same concept from the beginning til now. The establishment of this resort was originally from the owner's sake of wishing to have a luxury escape in the nature with his family. Therefore, he initially the concept of building all sorts of activities his kid would love to do, and while they are having fun in the nature, he and his wife could also have a relaxing time of their own at any lounge nearby.
